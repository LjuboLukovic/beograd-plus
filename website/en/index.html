Belgrade Bus Tickets

Beograd+ is a mobile app that enables you to buy bus tickets for public transportation in Belgrade, Serbia. Application is compatible with new Belgrade Plus (Beograd Plus) ticketing system that started in May 2023.

Application


You don't have to remember zones, numbers or codes, app has a simple UI. After you select ticket type you will be prompted to your default SMS application and you just click Send to activate your ticket.
