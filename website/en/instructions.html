BELGRADE PUBLIC TRANSPORT TICKETS

The purchase of tickets for public transportation is done by sending an SMS to the number 9011, and in the message text, we enter the ticket text depending on the type of ticket we want to purchase. There are several types of tickets: A ticket valid for 90 minutes, a daily ticket valid for 24 hours, a weekly ticket, and a monthly ticket. Additionally, the city is divided into two zones, Zone A and Zone B, for which separate tickets can be purchased, as well as the option to purchase a ticket valid for both zones.

There are several types of tickets:

TIME TICKET - 90 MINUTES

  • Zone A - price 50 RSD, ticket text for SMS message A90
  • Zone B - price 50 RSD, ticket text for SMS message B90
  • Zone C (A+B) - price 100 RSD, ticket text for SMS message C90

DAILY TICKET - 1 DAY

  • Zone A - price 120 RSD, ticket text for SMS message A1
  • Zone B - price 120 RSD, ticket text for SMS message B1
  • Zone C (A+B) - price 150 RSD, ticket text for SMS message C1

WEEKLY TICKET - 7 DAYS

  • Zone A - price 800 RSD, ticket text for SMS message A7
  • Zone B - price 800 RSD, ticket text for SMS message B7
  • Zone C (A+B) - price 1000 RSD, ticket text for SMS message C7

MONTHLY TICKET - 30 DAYS

  • Zone A - price 2200 RSD, ticket text for SMS message A30
  • Zone B - price 2200 RSD, ticket text for SMS message B30
  • Zone C (A+B) - price 3300 RSD, ticket text for SMS message C30
